App Name:
Talk Shuffle

Subtitle (30 chars max):
Pick a topic fast

Promotional Text:
From team building to 1:1 reflection, pick conversation prompts in seconds with 3D dice and curated card decks.

Description:
Talk Shuffle helps you choose a conversation topic quickly and naturally.
Roll a 3D dice for a random prompt, or use curated card decks designed for work and reflection.

Highlights
- 3D dice to select a topic at random
- Drag & drop to set your topic candidates
- Card decks for work and reflection (values, group discussion, self-reflection, 1:1)
- Player count and name setup
- Optional timer
- Session history stored on device
- Japanese / English support

Great for
- Team building and icebreakers
- 1:1s and personal reflection
- Casual chats and creative brainstorming

Privacy
Talk Shuffle works without account registration and does not send personal data to external servers.
(Session history is stored locally on your device.)
