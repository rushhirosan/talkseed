## Description (first comment / maker comment)
Talk Shuffle helps teams and friends choose a conversation topic quickly and naturally.

🎲 Roll a 3D dice for a random prompt
🃏 Use curated card decks for work and reflection
⏱ Optional timer for structured sharing
📱 Session history stored on device — no account needed
🌐 Japanese & English support

Great for:
- Team building & icebreakers
- Meeting check-ins and wrap-ups
- 1:1s and personal reflection
- Casual chats and creative brainstorming

v2.0 brings card decks (values, check-in/out, self-reflection, 1:1), session history, and a polished bilingual experience.

Privacy: No account registration. No personal data sent to external servers.
