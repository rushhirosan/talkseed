App Name:
Talk Seed

Short description (80 chars max):
Pick a topic fast with 3D dice and card decks for teams and 1:1s.

Full description:
Talk Seed helps you choose a conversation topic quickly and naturally.
Roll a 3D dice for a random prompt, or use curated card decks for work and reflection.

Highlights
- 3D dice to select a topic at random
- Drag & drop to set your topic candidates
- Card decks for work and reflection (values, check-in/out, self-reflection, 1:1)
- Player count and name setup
- Optional timer
- Session history stored on device
- Japanese / English support

Great for
- Team building and icebreakers
- Meeting check-ins and wrap-ups
- 1:1s and personal reflection
- Casual chats and creative brainstorming

Privacy
No account required. No personal data is sent to external servers.
(Session history is stored locally on your device.)
